Google Sheet Live Sync

The sheet is the app's home base: every log, date, and note change pushes automatically over wifi (APP LOG / APP ALL DATES / APP PARTS USED tabs), and photos are stored INSIDE the sheet on the APP PHOTOS tab. "Load From Google Sheet" pulls the master copy down — for a new or replacement device.

Backup & Restore

Backup copies all log data as text. Save it somewhere safe (email it to yourself). Restore pastes it back in — use this to move data to another device. NOTE: photos are too big for the text backup — they stay on this device only.
